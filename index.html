01 — About

What I do, in plain words.

Based in Asunción, Paraguay. GMT-3. Fluent async in English.

I help teams ship AI-powered web products — not demos, actual production systems that clinicians, students, and operators rely on every day.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of healthcare, LLMs, and complex front-end architecture. That means entity-grounded prompting to prevent hallucinations, streaming responses for real-time UX, accessibility-first UIs for users who can't afford friction, and the dozens of small decisions around a model that turn a cool demo into a product people trust.

Before the AI chapter, I built high-performance storefronts for Hollister and Abercrombie & Fitch, maintained the ZenBusiness platform, and shipped enterprise Salesforce solutions at Oktana. Different verticals, same obsession: make the product fast, accessible, and pleasant to work on.

I care about accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA), performance budgets, and writing code that the next engineer — probably me in six months — won't hate. I organize GDG Asunción and mentor juniors. Runner at heart.
